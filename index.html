How to Get Basic System Information in Windows

Follow these steps to gather and submit your system information:

Step 1: Right Click the Windows button on the taskbar and click "System"

Step 2: In the Settings window, go to "System" and then "About". Here, you will see details about your device, including CPU, RAM, and Windows version.

Step 3: Copy the information displayed in the "Device specifications" section (CPU, RAM, etc.) and paste it into the text box on the form.
